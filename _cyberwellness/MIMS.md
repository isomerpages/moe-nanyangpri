<p>What is MIMS?</p>
<p>MOE Identity Management System or “MIMS” is the user account generated
for students to access all MOE Centrally Provisioned Digital Tools like:</p>
<p>• Student iCON Email</p>
<p>• SLS</p>
<p>• AllEars</p>
